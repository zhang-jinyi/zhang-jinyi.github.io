Introduction [ORCID]

I am an Associate Professor and Master's Supervisor at the School of Information Science and Engineering, Shenyang Ligong University. I holds a Ph.D. in Engineering from Gifu University, Japan. I has received funding from the China Scholarship Council for doctoral and postdoctoral studies, as well as support from Japan's Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology (MEXT) and the Gifu International Exchange Center. Currently, I am a Special Researcher at Gifu University.

I serves as a program committee member for top AI conferences such as AAAI and ICLR and reviews for prestigious conferences and journals like NeurIPS, EMNLP, ICML, ACL, IEEE Transactions, and IEEE Access. I am also an editor for MDPI, a member of the Youth Work Committee of the Chinese Information Society, a regular member of the Japan Association for Language Processing, and a member of IEEE.

My research focuses on Artificial Intelligence, particularly Natural Language Processing and Large Language Models (especially Machine Translation), where he has extensive research and practical experience.
